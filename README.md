## Welcome!

This is your personal online store where your customers can browse your cologne collection, add items to their cart, and check out — all from one clean, professional website.

## How Your Store Works

Your website is made up of **3 simple services** — all free to start:

| Service | What It Does | Cost |
|---------|-------------|------|
| **GitHub Pages** | Hosts your website (makes it live on the internet) | Free |
| **Sanity** | Where you manage your products (add colognes, set prices, upload photos) | Free (up to 3 team members) |
| **Stripe** | Handles payments from your customers securely | Free (they take 2.9% + 30¢ per sale — you only pay when you make money) |

**Optional:** A custom domain name (like `www.colognesbyyurimio.com`) costs about $10–15/year. Without one, your site will be at `adam-lovell.github.io/colognes-by-yurimio`.

## What You Need To Do (3 Steps)

### Step 1: Create Your Sanity Account (Product Management)

This is where you'll add, edit, and remove colognes from your store.

1. Go to [sanity.io](https://www.sanity.io) and click **"Get started"**
2. Sign up with your Google account or email
3. Once you're in, send me your **Project ID** and **Dataset name** — I'll connect it to your website
4. After setup, you'll be able to log in any time to manage your products

**What you can do from Sanity:**
- Add new colognes (name, description, price, photos)
- Edit existing colognes
- Remove colognes you no longer sell
- Update your store's homepage content

### Step 2: Create Your Stripe Account (Payments)

This is how you get paid when someone buys a cologne.

1. Go to [stripe.com](https://stripe.com) and click **"Start now"**
2. Create your account and verify your identity (Stripe requires this for security)
3. Once you're in your Stripe Dashboard, go to **Developers → API Keys**
4. Send me your **Publishable Key** — I'll connect it to your website
5. That's it — when customers pay, the money goes directly to your bank account

**What you can do from Stripe:**
- See all your orders and payments
- Issue refunds if needed
- Track your revenue
- Set up bank account for payouts

### Step 3 (Optional): Get a Custom Domain Name

If you want your store at a clean web address like `www.colognesbyyurimio.com`:

1. Go to a domain registrar like [Namecheap](https://www.namecheap.com), [Google Domains](https://domains.google), or [GoDaddy](https://www.godaddy.com)
2. Search for the domain name you want and purchase it (~$10–15/year)
3. Send me the domain name — I'll connect it to your site

If you skip this step, your store will still work perfectly at the free GitHub address.
